# Asset provenance

Unless a file is listed otherwise below, original visual assets committed to
this repository are licensed under the repository's [MIT License](LICENSE).

Hermes Studio is an independent project. Its icons and characters are not the
Hermes Agent logo, do not represent official Hermes Agent branding, and are not
copied from Nous Research assets.

## Image-generated sources

The following original assets were generated with OpenAI's built-in ImageGen
during the Hermes Studio development session on 2026-07-16. The prompts asked
for an H-shaped office building icon and low-resolution, original pixel-art
office creatures. Project contributors selected, edited, resized, converted,
and/or removed backgrounds from the generated results.

| `apps/desktop/src-tauri/icons/hermes-studio-source.png` | Other files under `apps/desktop/src-tauri/icons/`; `apps/web/public/hermes-studio-icon.png` (copy of `icons/128x128.png`, used as the sign-in brand mark) | MIT |
| `apps/web/public/characters/hermes-studio-character-atlas-v4.webp` | Runtime sprite frames and profile portraits | MIT |

## Project-authored vectors

`apps/web/public/icon.svg` and `apps/web/public/icon-maskable.svg` are
project-authored simplified building icons and are licensed under MIT.

## Bundled fonts

`apps/web/public/fonts/noto-sans-jp/` contains Noto Sans JP (variable weight
100–900) served as unicode-range woff2 chunks, mirrored from Google Fonts.
Noto Sans JP is licensed under the SIL Open Font License 1.1; a copy of the
license is included at `apps/web/public/fonts/noto-sans-jp/OFL.txt`.

No Pokémon, game, or third-party character asset is included. Similarity to a
third-party character is unintended; please use the private security/contact
route described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for a rights-sensitive report.
