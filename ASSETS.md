# Asset provenance

Unless a file is listed otherwise below, original visual assets committed to
this repository are licensed under the repository's [MIT License](LICENSE).

Hermes Office is an independent project. Its icons and characters are not the
Hermes Agent logo, do not represent official Hermes Agent branding, and are not
copied from Nous Research assets.

## Image-generated sources

The following original assets were generated with OpenAI's built-in ImageGen
during the Hermes Office development session on 2026-07-16. The prompts asked
for an H-shaped office building icon and low-resolution, original pixel-art
office creatures. Project contributors selected, edited, resized, converted,
and/or removed backgrounds from the generated results.

## Project-authored vectors

`apps/web/public/icon.svg` and `apps/web/public/icon-maskable.svg` are
project-authored simplified building icons and are licensed under MIT.
